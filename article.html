Cases & Articles

IDS 2017, the most important dental exhibition in Europe, is finished. This IDS was the first in which we took part as exhibitors. This event was very important and significant for us.

It was the first our announcement abroud. Behind the scene there were 2 years of hard work: meetings, software development, 3 months of preparation for the exhibition: stand design, booklet preparation, presentation (which you can see here). We were one of 5 Ukrainian companies with 2,199 exhibitors. On this occasion, we also feel proud!

Of course, at the exhibition we have expected to acquire new users of our program (doctors and companies that produce implantology products). Our expectations were more than justified. But there was also a task – to check the concept of our program “for strength”: to find out how the companies and doctors from different countries will perceive the business model that we have chosen. 5 days, more than 190 meetings with the opinion leaders of various companies and more than 2000 contacts with dentists, confirmed – we are on the right way!

Thank you for your support and special thanks to everyone who visited our stand! Thank you for your feedback, likes and reposts – it’s very important for us.

We are paying attention to all your wishes, and we hope to become better, with your help.

Sincerely yours, ProDigiDent team
